RESEARCH INTERESTS

Pre-2020 (Harvard Period): Environmental and Cardiovascular Epidemiology

Nine-year research at Harvard focused on environmental epidemiology based on Framingham Heart Study (FHS). Main exposures: ambient air pollution, traffic emissions, meteorology and tobacco smoke; explored multi-system health damage and underlying molecular & imaging mechanisms, published as first/corresponding author in top environmental, cardiology and respiratory journals with over 400 total citations.

Post-2020 (Zhejiang University Period): AI-Driven Aging Assessment and Multi-Modal Health Data Science

Four core directions: aging research, environmental epidemiology, AI-driven health prediction modeling, interdisciplinary research. Utilized Chinese domestic cohorts, NHANES, UK Biobank; integrated deep learning, machine learning and multi-omics for translational public health research.

Major Cohort Platform Development

Deputy Director of Joint Office, Healthy Zhejiang One Million People Cohort; built provincial-prefecture-site three-tier QC system; over 800,000 enrolled participants by May 2026; integrated genomic, imaging, clinical, environmental and lifestyle data for gene-environment aging interaction study.
